About the Conference

A Forum for Civil Engineering Research

The International Conference on Community Based Research and Innovations in Civil Engineering (CBRICE-2026) is designed to gather scientists, technocrats, scholars and young researchers of civil engineering to present the latest advances in the relevant fields.

The conference fosters an environment conducive to exchanging ideas and information, while providing an ideal platform to develop new collaborations and meet experts on the fundamentals, applications, and products of the highlighted fields.

Additionally, CBRICE-2026 will provide researchers with additional opportunities to gain exposure to scientific concepts and methods related to applications across different domains of Civil Engineering.

Collaborative Network

Build meaningful connections with researchers and practitioners across civil engineering disciplines worldwide.

SCOPUS Indexed Publication

All accepted full-length papers will be published in SCOPUS Indexed proceedings, ensuring global research visibility.

Interdisciplinary Scope

Covering five major domains — structural, transportation, water resources, environmental, and geotechnical engineering.

Conference Topics

Thrust Areas

Conference topics include, but are not limited to the following areas. Authors are encouraged to submit original research and review articles across these disciplines.

A

Structural Engineering

  • Life-cycle performance assessment and management
  • Structural health monitoring and control
  • Composite construction
  • Sustainable concrete materials and construction practices
  • Durability of concrete structures
  • Nanotechnology applications in construction
  • Utilization of construction wastes in concrete
B

Transportation Engineering

  • Highway design, construction and maintenance
  • Pavement materials and characterization
  • Pavement evaluation and management
  • Urban and regional transportation planning
  • Travel behavior modelling
  • Transport economics and policies
  • Sustainable transportation
  • Smart cities
C

Water Resources Engineering

  • Computational hydraulics
  • Hydraulic structures
  • Rainfall-runoff modeling
  • Regionalization of hydrological extremes
  • Dam safety analysis and inundation
  • Hydrogeology
  • Irrigation engineering
D

Environmental Engineering

  • Water and wastewater treatment
  • Waste management
  • Environmental modeling
  • Green and clean technologies
  • Sustainable technologies for safe environment
  • Air and plastic pollution
  • Climate change
  • IoT for smart buildings and energy environment
E

Geotechnical Engineering

  • Mining and geotechnics
  • Soil and rock mechanics and characterization
  • Transportation geotechnics, engineering and technology
  • Dams and tunneling
  • Engineering geology
  • Geoenvironmental related topics
  • Geohazards and risk assessment
  • Geotechnical infrastructure

SCOPUS Indexed Publication

All accepted full-length papers of the conference will be published in SCOPUS Indexed proceedings, providing international peer-reviewed recognition.
